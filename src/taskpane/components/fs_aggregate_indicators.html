Example Formula

=FS_AggregateIndicators(
"VOO"
, "60"
)

Arguments

symbol
Any symbol
resolution
Supported resolution includes
1, 5, 15, 30, 60, D, W, M

Results

Buy
Number of buy signals.
Neutral
Number of neutral signals.
Sell
Number of sell signals.
Signal
Aggregate signal.
Adx
Adx reading.
Trending
Whether market is trending or going sideway.
